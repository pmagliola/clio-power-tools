## Full Description

Stop losing billable hours to Clio's time tracking workflow.

Clio Tools adds a floating timer directly inside Clio Manage. One click to start tracking. Log the time entry to any matter — without switching tabs, without losing your place, without breaking your focus.

**How it works**

A small timer widget sits in the corner of every page in Clio. Click Start to begin tracking, Pause when you step away, and Log when you're ready to record the entry. A simple dialog lets you add a description and confirm the matter — then it posts directly to Clio via the official API.

**Features**

- Floating timer that stays visible across all Clio pages
- Drag the widget anywhere on screen
- Start, pause, and reset with one click
- Log time entries directly to any Clio matter
- Works with all Clio regions: US, AU, EU, and CA
- Connects via Clio's official OAuth 2.0 API — your credentials are never stored by us
- 14-day free trial included

**Who is this for?**

Attorneys, paralegals, and legal assistants who use Clio Manage and find themselves losing track of time or batching entries at the end of the day. If you've ever thought "I need to log that but I'll do it later" — this extension is for you.

**Pricing**

- 14-day free trial, no credit card required
- $9.99/month or $84.99/year (2 months free) after your trial

**Privacy**

Clio Tools does not collect or transmit your personal data. Your Clio OAuth token is stored locally in Chrome's secure storage. Time entries go directly from your browser to Clio's servers — they never pass through our systems. Full privacy policy at cliotools.com/privacy.html.
